The Angular 2 workshop

Register now, the spots are limited! English and Portuguese dates to be announced :)

Grab your spot

Ever wanted to dive into the constantly-evolving world of Front-End frameworks? Felt quite lost not knowing where to start? Looks like your chance to learn Angular (aka Angular 2), which is currently the hottest and most sofisticated technology for web development, has finally come! Its final version officially came out in January, making Angular a great bet for projects within the next couple years.

It is free

Nothing quite like spreading the knowledge the internet and communities in general give us, eh? With this idea in mind, @anazard (me) invites anyone interested in learning, either professional or enthusiast developer.

How will it work?

Roughly, it will be an online workshop held through Google Hangouts (athough suggestions for other platforms are welcome). We will see the basics of Angular, do a few exercises, and work on an exciting Pokédex fed by the PokéAPI.
We will cover everything needed in order to take the first steps with the framework, including (but not limited to):
  • Modules and components
  • Requests and services
  • Routing
  • Command line interface
